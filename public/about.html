About

FamilyTree 3.0 is built to respect how families really grow.

This system was designed for multi-generational families that want both flexibility and control. It keeps records consistent without locking people out of their own stories.

Our Focus

We focus on accuracy, ownership, and cultural context. Roles help keep data safe, while the relationship dictionary supports Tamil naming traditions.

What makes it different

  • Role-based editing for clean data.
  • Person-to-user linking for accountability.
  • Powerful relationship engine built in.
